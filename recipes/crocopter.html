Crocopter

Description

Hehe a remote controlled crocopter , The small size and low inertia of crocopter allows use of a particularly simple flight control system, which has greatly increased the practicality of the small crocopter in this application.

Ingredients

  • Crocs (Throw away the other one)
  • Controller
  • Mounting pads
  • Batteries
  • Propellers
  • ESCs
  • USB key

Steps

  1. Create a frame
  2. Connect the Propellers
  3. Wipe it with oil
  4. Wait for rain
  5. Fly the crocopter
