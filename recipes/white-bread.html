White Bread

Description

Bread is a soft but dense sweet white bread made from a rich, low-yeast dough. This dough will take its time to rise, but it can be prepared up to 1 day in advance and slow-proofed in the refrigerator, which will develop the dough’s flavor. This recipe fits a standard Pullman loaf pan, so it makes a lot. Serve the bread in nice thick slices alongside some obe ata and scrambled eggs, or butter and toast it to enjoy dunked into milk tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  •  ½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Place water, sugar, salt, oil, bread flour and yeast into pan of bread machine.
  2. On White Bread setting. Cool on wire racks before slicing.
