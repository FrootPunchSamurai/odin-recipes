Iron "Hot" Dog

Description

A Dish for the people with an iron will (maybe an iron teeth), It's indeed a very hot dog

Ingredients

  • Iron ores
  • Furnace
  • 1 cup lava - Optional
  • Iron bread
  • Fire safety kit

Steps

  1. Forge the iron
  2. Turn it into a sausage shaped thingy
  3. Don't burn your hands
  4. Place the iron sausage on the iron bread using a pair of tongs
  5. Hot and crispy
